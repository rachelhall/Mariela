Mariela is an indie pop band formed in 2017 by Nashville songwriter and producer, Rachel Hall. Mariela tunes are characterized by introspective melancholy lyrics packed in candy coated hooks so sweet they’ll make your teeth hurt. Mariela’s newest release, Brandy, is the first track on the band’s upcoming full length record, slated to release fall of 2024.
